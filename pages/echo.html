[ KNOWLEDGE_BASE & DISCLAIMERS ]

NOTICE: SOME DATA MAY BE MASKED BY BROWSER SHIELDS OR ISP ROUTING.

Why is hardware data capped?

Modern browsers use "Anti-Fingerprinting" to protect your privacy. High-end hardware is often reported as standard values (e.g., 8GB RAM) to prevent unique tracking.

How accurate is geolocation?

IP-based geolocation identifies the location of your ISP's datacenter. Behind CGNAT or VPNs, the reported city may be far from your actual location.

Adblock Detection

Strict browsers like Brave handle adblocking natively. We probe common ad domains; if the request fails, we assume filtering is active.
